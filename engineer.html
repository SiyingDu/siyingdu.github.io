Instructional Design | Blended Learning: Softskills for Engineering Student

Globalization and the development of new technology is challenging the current engineering education, as conventional engineering education is focusing purely on the hard skills development of students yet didn’t provide any systematic training for the students in field of soft skills such as team work, communication skills and so on. Meanwhile from the engineering industry’s perspectives, engineers require higher and higher level skills in communication as well as other soft skillsets to fulfill their job tasks. However, many undergraduate engineering programs still fail to provide students with systematic instruction regarding interpersonal communication competencies therefore there is still a big gap between the competencies needed from the industry and the communication skillset of the student after trained from schools. This design project is to design a professional communication skills program for undergraduate engineering students, which takes advantage of both web-based learning and mobile learning together, merging formal learning and informal learning, to provide a seamless learning environment for learners.
